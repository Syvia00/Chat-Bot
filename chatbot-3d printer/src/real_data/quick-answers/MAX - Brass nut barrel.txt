MAX Brass nut barrel
 If there are issues with the platform holder, then you may be able to change just the brass nut, and the calibration screw and washers.

Please borrow one temporarily from another printer to check if this fixes the problem. Otherwise, you would need the entire platform holder which costs a bit more.

The brass nut on the max platform holder is PN/01154 it is dealer price $10usd, and rrp $15.50usd.
The screw is PN/02849 and is dealer price $0.80 usd, rrp $1usd.
The stainless washer is PN/00185 and is dealer price $0.40 usd, rrp $0.50usd.
The brass washer is PN/03251 and is dealer price $0.80 usd, rrp $1usd.

If you need the entire platform holder, it is PN/02480 and is dealer price $185 usd, rrp $285usd.
