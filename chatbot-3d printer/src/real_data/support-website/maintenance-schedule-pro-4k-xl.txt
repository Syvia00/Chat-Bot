General housekeeping 

Asiga printers require very little preventative maintenance.It’s best practice to keep your Asiga printer as clean as possible. This means cleaning up resin drops or spills with IPA (Isopropyl Alcohol) but also preventing dust build up.Asiga recommends using a microfibre cloth with cold water to wipe down the outside of the printer.Follow all instructions and safety advisories mentioned below.

PRO 4K XL Basin Glass
Keeping the basin glass clean on your PRO 4K XL is extremely important.The projector will reflect the image through this window & smudges will impact it’s performance.
