Why are there tags or flakes on my prints?
If you’re experiencing extra cured material on your prints, this page will explain why this is happening & how to prevent it.
See the following image https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/tags-flakes.jpg
See the following image https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/dirty-optical-path.jpg

Dirty basin
If the basin has smudges or cured resin over it, it will cause light scattering.Use a torch to enhance visibility of the glass.
See the following image https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/MAX-Smudgy-glass.jpg
The below video demonstrates the recommended way to clean the MAX basin glass.
See the following image https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/MAX-cleaning-basin.jpg
Dirty mirror
If this did not solve your problem, it is possible that the mirror beneath the basin is dirty.
Inspect the mirror underneath the glass basin. This can attract dust & may cause print defects.
See the following image https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/MAX-Dirty-Glass.jpg
If the glass is dirty:Remove the basin by unscrewing these 4 screws.Carefully blow away the dust. Avoid touching the mirror as it may smudge further.If the mirror is too dirty, consider replacing it by contacting Asiga Support.
