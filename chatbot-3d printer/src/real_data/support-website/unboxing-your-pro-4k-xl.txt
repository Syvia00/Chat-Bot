Before receiving your PRO 4K XL 

Choose a stable and clean workspace for your PRO 4K XL.A location away from any sources of vibration or dust is preferable.Operating your 3D printer next to a milling machine is not recommended and might cause performance issues.Purchase SuppliesNitrile GlovesPaper Towel98%+ Isopropyl Alcohol (IPA) Prepare a clean, dry space out of direct sunlight to store resin bottles and trays.Asiga recommends the Build Tray Storage Cases to safely to safely store Build Trays.
